About us

We have developed lots of robots since 2003.
There are 4 teams in IARC: Humanoid robot team, Mobile robot team, Manipulator robot team, and Computer vision team.
We are proactive participation in robot competition, like
Amazon Robotics Challenge (ARC), FIRA RoboWorld Cup, Hiwin Intelligence Robot Competition, etc.
Until now, we medalled more than a hundred at different robot competition.
